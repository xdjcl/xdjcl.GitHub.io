About Me

I (刘精昌) am currently a first year PhD candidate of the CSE Department in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. James T. Kwok. I received my Master’s Degree from CS department in the University of Science and Technology of China in 2019, supervised by Prof. Linli Xu. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Science from Xidian University in 2016. My research area is machine learning.
